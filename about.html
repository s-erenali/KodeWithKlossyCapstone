Work smarter. Increase Productivity.

Pomodoro Study Sesh has adopted the Pomodoro Technique developed in the late 1980s by Francesco Cirillo. This technique is used to better one's time managment, using a timer to break down work in intervals. Traditionally, there is 25 minutes of work followed by 5 minutes of break. Repeating this 4 times, a longer break of 45 minutes follows, which completes one full cycle of the Pomodoro Technique.


Using the Pomodoro Technique, Pomodoro Study Sesh hopes to provide the user with a simple, efficient way of working that eliminates distractions. Thank you! Enjoy!
